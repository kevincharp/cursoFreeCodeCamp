Easy Stir-Fried Rice

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 onion, sliced
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, corn)
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
